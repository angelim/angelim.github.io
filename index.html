Rails Redshift Replicator

May 13, 2016

Replicate your ActiveRecord tables to Redshift.

There are several reasons one should choose to replicate data to Redshift. Most of them are related to Data Analysis or Reporting activities. Maybe you’re using tools like Snowplow and loading events data directly into Redshift, but still needs to enrich those at runtime with your resources tables. Maybe you have a huge amount of data on your resources tables and were asked to build some complex reports. You may have even been tracking events on a local database and realized data analysis is too heavy for some database engines.

Mongoid Mirrored

May 24, 2011

Helper module for mirroring root documents in embedded collections.

Mongoid provides an intuitive interface to reference documents among different collections, but that comes at a cost. As MongoDB doesn’t support joins and relying heavily on relational data reads, that could be a problem for some applications. Unfortunately, Mongoid doens’t support embedding collections while maintaining an independent root collection and that is exactly the intention with mirrored documents.
